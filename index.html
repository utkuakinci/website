// about me

Who I Am

I'm a Computer Scientist and Mechatronics Engineer based in Bremen, Germany, with a background spanning robotics, embedded systems, and data science.

At DFKI's Space Robotics Team, I work on everything from spacecraft interface standardization to autonomous navigation for lunar micro-rovers.

Before research, I co-founded SKYTECH in Istanbul — a hardware startup where I learned first-hand what it takes to go from idea to prototype to business.

I speak English fluently, Turkish natively, and I'm learning German. I hold an MSc from Constructor University and a BSc in Mechatronics from Bahçeşehir University.
